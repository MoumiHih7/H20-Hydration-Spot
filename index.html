Our Water

Water with Purpose

H2O Hydration Co. bottles ultra-purified alkaline water that is pH 9+ to support optimal hydration and whole-body wellness. Alkaline water is believed to help neutralize acid in the body, improve hydration at the cellular level, and support athletic performance and recovery. Every drop is filtered through a multi-stage process to remove impurities and enhance mineral content, ensuring a crisp, clean taste with every sip.

Alkaline Water: pH 9+ for optimal hydration.

Eco-Friendly Aluminum Bottles: 100% recyclable and refillable.

Cold-Filled: Fresh, crisp taste preserved from source to sip.

About Us

A Purpose-Driven Bottled Water Brand

At H2O Hydration Co., hydration goes far beyond simply quenching thirst; it represents a deep commitment to enhancing both personal health and environmental sustainability. Drawing inspiration from the resilient and vibrant spirit of Tucson, AZ, the brand delivers a premium alkaline water experience thoughtfully designed to meet the needs of today’s health-conscious and eco-aware consumers. Their cold-filled, ultra-purified alkaline water is carefully formulated to help neutralize body acidity, support optimal hydration, assist in natural detoxification processes, and promote sustained energy levels throughout the day.

H2O Hydration Co. is a family-owned business proudly rooted in Tucson, Arizona. As members of this vibrant community, we’re deeply committed to promoting wellness, sustainability, and local partnerships. Every bottle we produce reflects our dedication to health, the environment, and the people we serve — because we don’t just do business here, we live here too.
